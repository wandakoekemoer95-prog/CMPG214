# CMPG214 - Assignemnt 1: Designing Yourself

My name is Wanda Koekemoer. I am a teacher, currently completing a B.Sc. in Information Technology at the Nort-West University.

This assignemnt taught me how to explain, and find patterns in how people think. Now I am applying those same skills in a new context — technology, data, and systems.
I am working towards a role as a Business Analyst, where both worlds meet: people and process, communication and code.

This assignment asked me to design a personal identity system — not to produce something polished, but to think carefully about who I am and how to represent that visually under constraint.

My logomark is two keyboard keys: [WK] + [</>]
- [WK], my initials, my identity
- [</>], my foundation in IT and code
- The +, the transition between who I was and who I am becoming

This is not a brand. It is a visual argument about a person in motion.
